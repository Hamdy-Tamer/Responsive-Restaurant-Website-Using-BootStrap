Our Story

Since 1985, L'Étoile du Soir has been redefining fine dining with innovative culinary techniques and the finest ingredients.

Founded by Chef Laurent Dubois, our restaurant has earned three Michelin stars and has been consistently ranked among the world's top dining destinations. We believe in creating unforgettable experiences through meticulous attention to detail and a passion for gastronomic excellence.
